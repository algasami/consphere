What is consphere?

Consphere is a static website making use of webpack, typescript and various packages to aid its development. It is almost framework-less in pursuit of a simple yet versatile website. It is a small hobby project developed occasionally.

Why go framework-less?

While frameworks such as Angular and Next.js have been proven to be extremely powerful and convenient, speaking of my personal experience working with them, I find myself knowing nothing about the web when focusing on the more low-level aspect of it. I argue that it is largely owing to the fact that these frameworks have layers of abstraction that make things more "dev-friendly". They are well-designed, nevertheless, having possibilities of creating illusions of the underlying mechanisms of the web behind the hood. The above-mentioned reason is why I choose to go framework-less on this specific project.
